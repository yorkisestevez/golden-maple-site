Outdoor Lighting in Craighurst, thoughtfully designed and built

We design lighting that supports safe movement, calm evening use, and a refined atmosphere around the home.

Lighting is treated as part of the overall landscape design. We balance functional path lighting with warm ambient layers to keep the space composed at night.

Installations prioritize clean wiring routes, durable connections, and fixture placement that avoids glare while highlighting key features.

What we build

  • Path and step lighting for safe circulation.
  • Feature lighting for trees, walls, and entry elements.
  • Ambient patio lighting for evening use.
  • Edge lighting for grade changes and transitions.
  • Discreet transformer and wiring placement.
  • Fixture planning aligned with the landscape design.

Our process

  1. Structured intake to confirm fit and project goals.
  2. Lighting plan and fixture strategy built into the layout.
  3. Installation with clean routing and final nighttime calibration.
