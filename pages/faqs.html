We provide a 5-year workmanship warranty on all structural installations. Additionally, all manufacturer warranties on materials (such as stone pavers or composite decking) are fully passed on to you, often providing protection for 25+ years.

We use a simple 2-step deposit structure to begin work immediately.

Step 1: $750 to begin planning, site analysis, and concept development.

Step 2: $750 upon delivery of the final design and construction estimate.

The full $1,500 is credited 100% toward your construction contract if you proceed with the build. Stand-alone design value is typically $3,000 - $5,000.

Most structural projects (decks over 24" high, retaining walls, etc.) require permits. As part of our service, we handle the entire permit application and engineering phase for you.
