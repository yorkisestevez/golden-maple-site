A Clear, Predictable Process — From First Call to Final Result

Predictability. Clarity. Quality. A structured approach to complex outdoor construction projects.

01

Initial Compatibility

We begin by reviewing your project goals through our intake form.

This ensures our expertise aligns with your vision before we move forward.

Investment Security

Every phase is documented and signed so workmanship and materials remain aligned with the approved plans.

Consistent oversight from permitting through completion reinforces the disciplined build standard our clients expect.

We intentionally limit the number of projects we take on each season to maintain this standard.

Design Planning

We resolve scope, layout, materials, and constructability before breaking ground so every decision is considered. Early coordination removes surprises and lets you move forward with confidence.

Intentional planning keeps the build aligned with the home’s architecture while honoring the high standards of the region’s climate and site conditions.

02

Design & Planning Phase

Our planning discipline resolves every direction before we build. Two paths guide the work, matching the project type to a tailored process.

Some projects solve a focused issue — drainage, grading, water management, or structural corrections.

These require deliberate coordination. We assess conditions, confirm scope, and align trades so the agreed outcome is delivered with discipline.

Full Backyard & Design-Led Projects are guided by a deep design phase.

Proper planning ensures layouts, material selections, and coordination are documented so execution mirrors the intent.

Clients gain the clarity to proceed knowing every detail has been coordinated before breaking ground.

03

Coordination & Contract

We handle permits, utility locates, scheduling, and fixed-price contracts so nothing is left to assumption.

All documents are shared in your personal project folder for full transparency.

04

Construction & Handoff

Our crew builds with precision, following the approved plan exactly — no shortcuts, no improvisation.

Weekly communication ensures you are always up to date on progress.

Build Review

The final review confirms constructability, site readiness, and adherence to the drawn plan so the handoff is smooth. We check every system, coordination, and detail with the same discipline used to begin the project.

This accountability ensures the project closes with the professionalism you expect from a managed, design-driven build.
