Thoughtfully Built Outdoor Spaces

We plan and build outdoor spaces with care, attention to detail, and long-term durability in mind. Our focus is simple — do the work properly and build things that last.

A Team You Can Rely On

At Golden Maple Landscaping, we approach outdoor construction the same way we’d want our own homes handled — carefully planned, well built, and clearly communicated.

We work with homeowners who value quality, clear expectations, and a smooth experience from start to finish.

We take on a limited number of projects each season so we can stay focused, organized, and hands-on throughout the build.

Our Work, Backed by Warranty

Our Work, Backed by Warranty

We stand behind our work. All hardscape and structural projects include a 5-year workmanship warranty covering installation and craftsmanship.

Manufacturer warranties on materials are also passed directly to you, providing additional long-term coverage.

What Matters to Us

  • Doing the job right. Careful planning and proper installation matter.
  • Building for the long term. We focus on durability, not shortcuts.
  • Clear communication. We believe homeowners should always know what to expect.
