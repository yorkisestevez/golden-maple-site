Bradford Backyard Regrade & Outdoor Living Space (2021)

Bradford, Ontario

Backyard regrading, drainage correction, and outdoor living space.

Project Overview

This project involved a full backyard regrade and drainage correction to address long-standing water issues and an unusable slope. The goal was to create a stable, functional outdoor living space that could support everyday use while properly managing site conditions.

The Challenge

The existing subbase drainage was poor, causing water to collect across the yard and limiting how the space could be used. Grade changes across the property made traditional surface solutions ineffective without addressing the underlying conditions.

The Solution & Execution

Infrastructure & Site Work

  • Full backyard regrading
  • Installation of multiple drainage lines placed strategically to manage water movement
  • Retaining wall construction to stabilize the slope

Drainage infrastructure was prioritized early in the build. Multiple drain pipes were installed in specific locations to move water efficiently away from the living space and prevent future saturation. A retaining wall was introduced to resolve the slope and create usable, level areas.

Outdoor Living Elements

  • Pergola with power run for lighting and future use
  • Structurally reinforced privacy wall designed to support an outdoor television
  • Emerald cedar planting for privacy

The project was planned to balance performance, durability, and the client’s vision for the space. Electrical was run to the pergola at the client’s request, and the privacy wall was reinforced structurally to accommodate an outdoor TV without future modification.

Outcome

Completed in 2021, the project transformed an underperforming yard into a stable, functional outdoor environment. Proper drainage, grade correction, and structural planning allowed the space to be used comfortably while protecting it from long-term water issues.
