Barrie Backyard Transformation

Overview

This project involved a full backyard transformation with multiple slope changes and existing drainage challenges. From the outset, water management and grade correction were critical considerations.

Site Conditions

  • Multiple elevation changes
  • Ongoing drainage issues
  • Water movement across the property

These conditions required careful planning to avoid future settlement or surface water problems.

Planning Approach

The scope was intentionally developed to align with a client-defined budget. Rather than attempting to solve every condition at once, the project focused on the most impactful areas. Expectations were clearly established early so decisions could be made confidently throughout the build.

Execution

Drainage coordination and grade transitions were handled with long-term performance in mind. Each area was constructed to support durability, stability, and ease of maintenance.

Outcome

The completed space significantly improves usability while addressing underlying site issues. The project demonstrates how thoughtful planning and clear communication can deliver strong results within defined constraints.
