Understanding Outdoor Project Investment Levels

Planning a high-quality outdoor living project starts with understanding how projects are typically scoped and invested in.

At Golden Maple Landscaping, every project is custom-designed. However, over time, clear patterns emerge in how outdoor living projects are planned, built, and budgeted. This guide exists to provide clarity — not quotes — so you can better understand what level of project aligns with your goals before reaching out.

The investment ranges outlined below are not packages or fixed scopes. They are starting points designed to help homeowners self-select the appropriate level of design, complexity, and execution.

This approach allows for:

  • More productive conversations
  • Clear expectations from the start
  • Better alignment between vision, scope, and investment

If one of these levels feels like the right fit, the next step is a design consultation where details, feasibility, and direction are confirmed.

Essential Outdoor Upgrade

Starting investment: $25,000–$35,000

Focused improvements to a single outdoor area, designed and built with long-term performance, clarity, and quality in mind.

Signature Outdoor Living

Starting investment: $45,000–$65,000

Cohesive outdoor living spaces where layout, elevation, and flow are intentionally designed as a complete system.

Estate / Resort-Style Outdoor Build

Starting investment: $150,000+

Fully designed outdoor environments built as cohesive extensions of the home, often involving multiple zones and high-touch execution.

Important to Know

  • Every Golden Maple project is custom
  • Final investment depends on site conditions, materials, and design scope
  • Ranges are educational starting points only

If one of these investment levels feels aligned, the next step is a design consultation to confirm scope, feasibility, and direction.
