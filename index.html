Our Approach to Design

Every project begins with clarity.

We take the time to understand the site, the architecture, and how the space is meant to be used before construction begins. Design decisions are intentional. Layouts are resolved early. Details are coordinated, not improvised.

Our work is professionally managed from concept through completion, with careful attention to structure, drainage, and long-term performance. The result is an outdoor environment that feels cohesive, durable, and timeless.

Typical Investment Ranges

Every outdoor living project is unique. The ranges below are provided for planning purposes only and reflect professionally built projects completed to a high durability and warranty standard.

  • Entry-Level Patios (simple layouts, minimal elevation change): $25,000+
  • Custom Patios & Walkways (premium materials, thoughtful layout): $40,000 – $90,000+
  • Complex Outdoor Living Spaces (steps, drainage, lighting, multiple features): $90,000 – $160,000+
  • Comprehensive Design-Build Projects (multiple zones, integrated features): $140,000+

Final investment depends on site conditions, access, drainage requirements, materials selected, and overall project complexity. All projects are custom planned and built to perform in Ontario’s freeze–thaw climate.
